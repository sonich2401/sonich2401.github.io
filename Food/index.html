Food Insecurity is a Problem ...

Food insecurity is when someone is not sure where their next meal will come from. While food insecurity is a unfortunate situation for anyone, it has severely affected the children of our community that face food insecurity. Food Insecurity affects the people around us even if its not obvious.

What is this Website All About?

This website is a way to spread awareness and explain research conducted by me. I have also compared my results to other researchers for a better understanding of the scope of this problem. 

Methods

To conduct my research, distributed a Google Forms survey with several questions that would give us some information to see how food-insecurity affects our local area. I also interviewed the leader for Open Hands Larae Tjoelker on how prevalent the problem of food-insecurity is in Whatcom County.

See Research Results

What Will My Research Accomplish?

I hope that my research can be used to bring awareness to Whatcom County of the children who are suffering through food-insecurity. I expect future researchers might use my work for future findings to compare to see if the problem of food-insecurity has grown or has been declining. This can help leaders in our community to see the severity of the situation and act accordingly to resolve the problem.
