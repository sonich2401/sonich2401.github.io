Castle Flags

0x08-0x0B uint32

  • 0x08: Castle Stars - These are stars from tods and Mips.
    Bits
    1. Toad Star HMC
    2. MPIS 16 Star
    3. Toad Star Upstairs
    4. Toad Star TTC
    5. MIPS 50 star
  • 0x09: Castle Flag 1
    Bits
    1. Cap Stolen By Snowman
    2. Cap Stolen By Brid
    3. Cap Stolen By Monkey
    4. 50 Star Door Open
  • 0x0A: Castle Flag 2
    Bits
    1. DDD Portal Moved
    2. Castle Moat Drained
    3. Princess Secret Slide Door Unlocked
    4. Whomps Fortress Door Unlocked
    5. Cool Cool Mountain Door Unlocked
    6. Jolly Rodger Bay Door Unlocked
    7. Bowser 1 Star Door Unlocked
    8. Bowser 2 Star Door Unlocked
  • 0x0A: Castle Flag 2
    Bits
    1. Vaild Game
    2. Wing Cap Switch
    3. Metal Cap Switch
    4. Vanish Cap Switch
    5. Bowser 1 Key
    6. Bowser 2 Key
    7. Basement Door Unlocked
    8. Upstairs Door Unlocked
